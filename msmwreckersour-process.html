How we works

With our great service, you can sell your damaged or non-running car hassle in 4 simple steps.

Fill in your details or call us

Call us at 0409 946 080/0409 946 080 or fill in the form and a member of our friendly team will be in contact with you to confirm your details and organise a vehicle inspection at a time and location that convenient for you.

Host an inspection

Our experienced buyer will come to your location and evaluate the vehicle. This process only takes about 15 minutes and allows you to get on with your day’s activities with minimal interruption.

Get paid instantly

Once our buyer has inspected the vehicle, we will then make you an offer. If you accept the offer, we will pay you directly to your chosen bank account.

Pick up your vehicle

Once the transaction complete, we will then arrange pick up once again at time and location that is suitable for you.
